# FAQs

**Q: Will I be notified when a top-up is added to my account?**

A: Once a top-up is successfully processed, it is recorded with a unique Top-Up ID and reflected in your education account balance and Transaction History.

**Q: Can I request a top-up myself, or is it only done by the school?**

A: Top-ups are managed solely by school administrators; there is no self-service option for account holders to trigger them.

**Q: Where can I see how much top-up I've received over time?**

A: You can view your top-up history in your "Transaction History" (Education Account ledger), where entries are listed chronologically with the most recent at the top.

**Q: Where should I go when I want to pay my bills?**

A: Tap the bill notification or select "Payment" on the sidebar to open the Payment Dashboard, which shows your balance, current bills, and available services.

**Q: What payment plan options do I have when paying for a course?**

A: After enrolling, you can choose "Pay Now" or "Installment." If the course is eligible for a Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS), you may also offset the fee using a voucher.

**Q: If I choose to pay online, what payment methods are available?**

A: You can pay using your Education Account balance, online payment, or a combination of both. Supported methods include PayNow, Alipay, and Visa/MasterCard.

**Q: Does choosing "Pay Now" affect my regular monthly billing cycle?**

A: No. A "Pay Now" payment immediately marks the course fee as "Paid" and is independent of your monthly bill.

**Q: What happens if my "Pay Now" payment fails due to insufficient funds?**

A: If your selected account or card lacks funds, you can go back to the course and choose the button 'Continue Your Payment' and choose a suitable payment method to complete the transaction.

**Q: How does the installment plan work?**

A: When selecting "Installment," you can choose a plan (e.g., 3 or 6 months) to spread the course cost across your monthly bills.

**Q: Why do I get one consolidated bill instead of separate bills for each course?**

A: We combine all monthly installments and deferred fees into one consolidated bill, with each charge listed as a separate line item for transparency.

**Q: Will I be notified when my monthly bill is ready?**

A: Yes, you will receive a notification at the beginning of each month as soon as your consolidated bill is generated.

**Q: How can I view my upcoming bills?**

A: You can go to the Payment Dashboard and tap "View bills and pay" to see a detailed breakdown of your current installments and any outstanding charges.

**Q: When I click "Pay" on the Bill Page, how does the system decide my options?**

A: The system automatically checks your Education Account balance. If the balance is zero or insufficient, you will be prompted to choose either "Request Deferment" or "Online Payment."

**Q: Can I pay using my Education Account balance?**

A: Yes.
* **Full Balance**: If your balance covers the full amount, select "Education Account" to pay immediately.
* **Split Payment**: If your balance covers only part of the bill, select "Education Account + Online" to use your remaining balance and complete the rest with your preferred payment method.

**Q: How do I pay online if my balance is insufficient?**

A: You can continue to pay the remaining amount via Visa/MasterCard. Once the payment provider confirms the transaction, your bill will be marked as paid.

**Q: If I retry a failed online payment, what happens?**

A: If your payment fails, you can try again on the payment information page, provided your 15-minute session window is still active.

**Q: Where can I see the status of my past bills?**

A: Your "My Bill" page lists all past bills, showing the month, total amount, and status (Paid, Unpaid, or Deferred).

**Q: What determines whether a bill shows as Paid, Unpaid, or Deferred?**

A:
* **Paid**: The bill is fully paid off.
* **Unpaid**: The bill is due and has not been paid.

**Q: If I use "Pay Now" for a course, how does it appear in my history?**

A: It appears as a separate "Paid" line item under the month it occurred, distinct from your consolidated monthly bill.
