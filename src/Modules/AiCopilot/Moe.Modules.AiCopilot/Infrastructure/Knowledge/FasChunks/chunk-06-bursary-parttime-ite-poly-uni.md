# Government Bursary — Part-Time Students (wef Academic Year 2026)

Applies to part-time students at **ITE, Polytechnics, and Autonomous Universities** (Arts Institutions are not listed for the part-time track in source material).

## Higher Education Bursary (HEB) — part-time

**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI ≤ $5,500, or monthly PCI ≤ $1,375

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (part-time) | $350 |
| Diploma (part-time) | $1,050 |
| Undergraduate (part-time) | $2,800 |

**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI $5,501–$9,000, or monthly PCI $1,376–$2,250

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (part-time) | $800 |
| Diploma (part-time) | $1,800 |
| Undergraduate (part-time) | *Not present in source extract for this tier — do not state a figure; use fallback policy* |

**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI $9,001–$12,000, or monthly PCI $2,251–$3,000

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (part-time) | $380 |
| Diploma (part-time) | $750 |
| Undergraduate (part-time) | *Not present in source extract for this tier — do not state a figure; use fallback policy* |

## How to apply
Approach the financial aid office at the student's institution.
