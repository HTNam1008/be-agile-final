# MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS) — Junior College (JC) / Centralised Institute (CI) Level

## Eligibility criteria (effective January 2026)
A student qualifies if **either**:
- Monthly Gross Household Income (GHI) is **$4,000 or less**, OR
- Monthly Per Capita Income (PCI) is **$1,000 or less**

(PCI = Monthly GHI ÷ number of household members)

## Benefits at JC/CI level (effective January 2026)
Vary depending on the scheme

## How to apply
- Apply online, or download the MOE FAS application form / collect a copy at the institution, and submit the completed form directly to the institution.
- **No application cut-off date** — applications can be submitted at any time.
- If an applicant has children/siblings in different Government or Government-aided institutions, only **one** application form needs to be submitted, to any one of the institutions.

## Additional financial assistance
- Students can approach their institution for **school-based financial assistance**, evaluated case-by-case.
- Low-income households can apply separately for subsidised broadband and/or laptop/tablet under **IMDA DigitalAccess@Home**.
