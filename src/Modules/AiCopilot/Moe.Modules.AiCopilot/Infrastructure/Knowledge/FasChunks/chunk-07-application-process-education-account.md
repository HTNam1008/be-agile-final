# How to Apply for FAS / Manage Education Account Deductions

## Application channels
- Account Holders and students apply for financial assistance schemes through the **e-Service Portal**.
- There is **no application cut-off date** — applications can be submitted at any time during the year.
- If a household has more than one child/dependent at different Government or Government-aided institutions, only **one** application needs to be submitted, to any one of the institutions.

## AI-assisted application (e-Service Portal)
Per platform design:
1. The Account Holder can ask the in-portal AI assistant questions about e-Service Portal functionality. The assistant will ask follow-up questions to clarify context before answering.
2. If the AI assistant cannot answer a question, it should say so and offer to escalate (see chunk-02 fallback policy) rather than guess.
3. The AI assistant can **automatically pre-fill the FAS application form** using the Account Holder's existing profile data plus information gathered from the assistant conversation, reducing manual data entry.

## Education Account and fee deduction
- Once approved for FAS, eligible fee subsidies and bursary amounts are applied against the student's course fees, payable from their **Education Account**.
- Where a balance shortfall exists after FAS subsidy/bursary application, the remaining amount is payable by the Account Holder via the supported payment methods on the e-Service Portal.
- **Terminology note:** any reference elsewhere to "PSEA" deduction or consent should be understood as referring to the **Education Account** on this platform — do not present PSEA as a separate account or scheme to the user.

## Timing of approval
If an application is approved by the end of a given month, the FAS benefit generally takes effect from the **following month** — provided the application was accurate and complete.

## Re-application / duration
FAS is granted until **December of the given year**. Students must re-apply for the following academic year, since household income and family circumstances may change year to year.

## If income criteria are not met
The Account Holder can approach the student's institution to be considered for **institution-based financial assistance**, assessed case-by-case. This platform does not adjudicate institution-based assistance directly.
