# Tiered Fee Subsidy at Secondary/JC Level (Scheme Name Pending Verification)

## Income-tiered structure (effective January 2026)
### 1. MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)
Monthly gross household income (GHI) ≤$4,000, or a monthly per capita income (PCI) ≤$1,000
Junior College (JC)/Centralised Institute (CI) level 
- 100% subsidy of school and miscellaneous fees 
- $1,600 annual bursary (inclusive of public transport subsidy)

### 2. MOE Independent School Bursary (ISB) 
- Tier GHI $4,001 - $5,500 or PCI $1,001 - $1,375: Student pays same fee as student studying in Government and Government-Aided Schools (GGAS)
- Tier GHI $5,501 - $9,000 or PCI $1,376 - $2,250: Student pays 1.5x GGAS fee
- Tier GHI $9,001 - $12,000 or PCI $2,251 - $3,000: Student receives subsidy of 33% of school and miscellaneous fees
### 3. Special Education Financial Assistance Scheme (SPED FAS)
•100% subsidy of school and miscellaneous fees • Free textbooks and school attire • Meal subsidies that pay for 10 meals per school week • Public transport subsidy of $21 per month or up to $252 per annum

## JC-level benefits at the top eligibility tier
- 100% subsidy of school, miscellaneous, and examination* fees
- $1,600 annual bursary (inclusive of public transport subsidy)
