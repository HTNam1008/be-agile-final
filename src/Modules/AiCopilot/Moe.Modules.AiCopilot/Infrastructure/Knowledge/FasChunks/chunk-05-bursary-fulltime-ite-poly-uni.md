# Government Bursary — Full-Time Students (wef Academic Year 2026)

Applies to full-time students at **ITE, Polytechnics, Arts Institutions, and Autonomous Universities**.

## Tier 1 — Higher Education Community Bursary (HECB)
**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI ≤ $4,000, or monthly PCI ≤ $1,000

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (full-time) | 100% fee subsidy + $1,850 cash bursary |
| Diploma (full-time) | $3,050 |
| Undergraduate (full-time) | $6,300 |
| Medicine (NUS), AY2026 intake (full-time) | $23,200 |
| Dentistry (NUS), AY2026 intake (full-time) | $22,750 |
| Medicine (NTU), AY2026 intake (full-time) | $26,100 |

## Tier 2 — Higher Education Bursary (HEB)
**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI $4,001–$5,500, or monthly PCI $1,001–$1,375

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (full-time) | $1,400 |
| Diploma (full-time) | $2,700 |
| Undergraduate (full-time) | $5,100 |
| Medicine (NUS) | $19,200 |
| Dentistry (NUS) | $18,750 |
| Medicine (NTU) | $22,100 |

**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI $5,501–$9,000, or monthly PCI $1,376–$2,250

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (full-time) | $700 |
| Diploma (full-time) | $2,050 |
| Undergraduate (full-time) | $3,250 |
| Medicine (NUS) | $15,200 |
| Dentistry (NUS) | $14,750 |
| Medicine (NTU) | $18,100 |

**Eligibility:** Monthly GHI $9,001–$12,000, or monthly PCI $2,251–$3,000

| Course type | Bursary amount |
|---|---|
| Nitec / Higher Nitec (full-time) | $490 |
| Diploma (full-time) | $950 |
| Undergraduate (full-time) | $1,350 |
| Medicine (NUS) | $9,200 |
| Dentistry (NUS) | $8,750 |
| Medicine (NTU) | $12,100 |

## Notes
- Bursary amounts for NUS Medicine/Dentistry and NTU Medicine differ by institution and intake year.
- After the bursary is applied, undergraduates at both NUS and NTU pay the same remaining fee once the maximum bursary amount has been used to offset fees.
- Combining the government bursary with additional university aid, a Medicine/Dentistry student qualifying for the top tier pays **no more than $5,000 per year** in tuition.

## How to apply
Approach the financial aid office at the student's institution to apply for government bursaries/loans.
